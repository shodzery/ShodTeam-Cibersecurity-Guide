## Examination and Preparation Notes

**CompTIA exams**: Available at Pearson VUE testing centers or online proctored. Performance-based questions supplement multiple choice.

**GIAC/SANS**: Open-book exams with a custom index; preparing the index is core to the study process. Online proctored (SENTINEL) or in person.

**Offensive Security (OSCP/OSEP)**: Practical, hands-on exams. No substitutes for lab practice. The official PEN-200 (OSCP) lab environment is essential preparation.

**ISC2 (CISSP)**: Computer Adaptive Testing (CAT) for English; 125–175 questions, 4 hours. Tests breadth across 8 domains rather than technical depth.

**GIAC vs. Offensive Security**: GIAC certifications are knowledge-validated (proctored multiple choice). Offensive Security certifications are skill-validated (practical). Both have value; they measure different things.
